What is AlgoWit

An AI research company that aims to develop smart algorithms for various applications including, among others, algorithmic trading, sports betting and fraud detection. AlgoWit invests in research and has multiple publications in the area of artificial data generation. AlgoWit also develops open source software that you can find at GitHub.
